Server Features

Everything you need to know before joining.

No Protected Bases

All bases are unprotected, so you could get raided or you could raid someone else, So hide your base well, its not against the rules to raid peoples bases if u can find them!

Active PVP

Once you leave the safe zone at spawn, PVP is enabled everywhere. We use combat-tagging plugins to ensure players cannot simply log out to escape a fight.

Player-Driven Shops

We have a ui shop that you can buy items easily. No real money is used you can earn it, Join to get a chance to get free money!

Premium Anticheat

We take fair play seriously. Our server runs a heavily configured, premium anticheat to block x-ray, combat hacks, and movement exploits so everyone has a fair fight.

Meet the Owners & Devs

Joining means you get a chance to meet a developer or owner of the game! you can ask them questions about server stuff, we are here to help.

Custom Dev Commands

We have custom dev-made commands that do stuff like setting homes, showing the UI shop menu, and instantly teleporting you back to spawn.
